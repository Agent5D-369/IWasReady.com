This Privacy Policy explains how IWasReady.com, operated by Rick Broider, collects, uses, and protects information when you use this website, take our free quizzes, subscribe to email communications, or purchase the audiobook.

By using this site, you agree to the practices described below. If you do not agree, please do not use the site.

This site is operated by Rick Broider, the author and creator of the Bridging Earth & Kanaria framework. For privacy inquiries, contact: rbroider@gmail.com

2. What We Collect

Email addresses. If you voluntarily subscribe to email communications through the form on this site, your email address is collected and processed by Substack, Inc. We do not store email addresses on our own servers. Substack's privacy policy governs how your data is handled: substack.com/privacy

Quiz responses. The Frequency Finder Quiz and the Starseed Leadership Signal Activation Quiz run entirely in your browser. No quiz answers or results are transmitted to or stored on our servers.

Server logs. This site is hosted on GitHub Pages (operated by GitHub, Inc., a Microsoft subsidiary). GitHub may collect standard server log data including IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited. See GitHub's privacy policy: GitHub Privacy Statement

Advertising data. If you arrived via a paid advertisement on Meta (Facebook/Instagram), Google, or another platform, those platforms may have placed cookies or tracking pixels on your device in accordance with their own privacy policies prior to your arrival. We are in the process of implementing ad tracking and will update this policy when any pixel or tag is added to this site.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your email with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

This site uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion, and opt out of sale. We do not sell personal information. To submit a request, email rbroider@gmail.com.
