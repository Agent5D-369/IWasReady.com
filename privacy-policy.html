Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how IWasReady.com, operated by Rick Broider, collects, uses, and protects information when you use this website, take our free quizzes, subscribe to email communications, or purchase the audiobook.

By using this site, you agree to the practices described below. If you do not agree, please do not use the site.

1. Who We Are

This site is operated by Rick Broider, the author and creator of the Bridging Earth & Kanaria framework. For privacy inquiries, contact: rbroider@gmail.com

2. What We Collect

Email addresses. If you subscribe through a form on this site, your email is collected and sent to Kit (formerly ConvertKit) for email delivery. We do not store email addresses on our own servers. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email we send.

Quiz responses. The Frequency Finder Quiz and the Starseed Leadership Signal Activation Quiz run entirely in your browser. No quiz answers are transmitted to or stored on our servers. Your result type may be passed as a URL parameter to the result page and, if you subscribe, included in your subscriber record to send you relevant content.

Attribution data. When you arrive at this site, we capture UTM parameters (source, medium, campaign, content, term) and advertising click identifiers (fbclid from Meta, ttclid from TikTok, gclid from Google) present in your URL. This data is stored in your browser's local storage under the keys iwr_attr_orig (first visit) and iwr_attr_last (most recent visit) and may be included in your subscriber record if you subscribe. It is used solely for understanding which content and campaigns are most helpful to our audience.

Analytics data. We use Google Analytics 4 (via Google Tag Manager) to understand aggregate site usage. Google Analytics collects anonymized data including pages visited, session duration, device type, and general geographic region. IP addresses are anonymized. For more, see Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Advertising measurement. We use the Meta Pixel and TikTok Pixel (loaded via Google Tag Manager) to measure how paid advertisements perform. These pixels may set cookies and collect data about your visit for ad attribution and retargeting purposes, governed by Meta's and TikTok's own privacy policies. See section 4 for links.

Server logs. This site is hosted on GitHub Pages (operated by GitHub, Inc., a Microsoft subsidiary). GitHub may collect standard server log data including IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited. See GitHub's privacy policy: GitHub Privacy Statement

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To send you content, transmissions, and updates related to Bridging Earth & Kanaria if you subscribed
  • To understand which channels, campaigns, and content pieces bring people to the site (attribution)
  • To measure the effectiveness of paid advertisements on Meta and TikTok
  • To understand aggregate site traffic and improve the quiz and site experience

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your email with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

First-party local storage. This site stores attribution data in your browser's local storage (not cookies) under the keys iwr_attr_orig and iwr_attr_last. This data contains UTM parameters and ad click identifiers from your visit and is used solely for internal attribution tracking. It does not leave your device except when included in a form submission if you choose to subscribe.

Third-party cookies. Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, and TikTok Pixel may set cookies in your browser. These are governed by the respective third-party privacy policies listed in section 4. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Disabling cookies may affect analytics measurement but will not affect your ability to use this site or take the quizzes.

Marketing and remarketing. Meta Pixel and TikTok Pixel are used for conversion measurement and may enable retargeting, meaning you may see ads for IWasReady.com on those platforms after visiting this site. You can opt out of interest-based advertising through your Meta or TikTok account settings or through industry opt-out tools such as optout.aboutads.info.

6. Your Rights — GDPR (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Request data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time (including unsubscribing from email)

To exercise these rights, email rbroider@gmail.com. For Kit-related data, you may also manage or delete your subscription directly by contacting us or using the unsubscribe link in any email.

7. Your Rights — CCPA (California Residents)

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion, and opt out of sale. We do not sell personal information. To submit a request, email rbroider@gmail.com.

8. Data Retention

Email addresses collected via Kit are retained until you unsubscribe. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email. Attribution data in local storage persists in your browser until cleared. Google Analytics data is retained per Google's standard retention settings (default 14 months). Server log data is retained per GitHub's standard policies.

9. Children's Privacy

This site is intended for adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us at rbroider@gmail.com and we will delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our tools and practices evolve. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the site following updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
